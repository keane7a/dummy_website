Our History

Understanding the history of your organisation can help you make strategic decisions and determine its future course by giving you important insights into its founding principles and significant turning points. This information strengthens commitment and alignment among your members by promoting a feeling of identity and continuity.

Founding and Origins

PPI Exeter was founded in Exeter on 17 November 2008

Evolved

On 20 December 2020, PPI Exeter was renamed to PPI Devon for an indefinite period

Trademark

Rougemont Castle which is featured on PPI Devon's badge, represents the start of Norman civilisation in Devon following their conquering of the region from the Romans in 1068.

Initial Goal

Uniting Indonesian students in Devon under the protection of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia.

Borderland

PPI Devon serves as the hub for all Indonesian students residing in Devon, Cornwall, and the neighbouring areas, including Plymouth.
